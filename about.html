The story behind the project

Our mission

Ekana was born from the dream of making the Internet's unlimited educational content truly usable through a most human principle: getting together.

We are on a mission to empower self-driven education through collaborative learning and ethical technology.

Our ultimate goal is to foster human flourishing through education!

Ekana vision is built upon the following premises

1. Motivation starts by learning what excites us

Learning something we find intrinsically motivating is a major premise for more fulfilling careers and learning paths! The Internet's wealth of content represents the potential for you to learn whatever you want. For Ekana, this is a chance for us to learn whatever you love.


2. Learning together enhances our potential

When people who are learning something they love get together, they all gain further motivation, inspiration and vision to go higher. Ekana’s small-team approach is born from the understanding that collaboration is a skill we must cultivate in the path to flourishing in any field!


3. We love to progress together

Enabling deep relationships, collaboration and mutual support is one of the greatest roles that technology can play for human beings. In Ekana, we celebrate and support human love for cooperation, inclusion and meaningful connection across our community.


4. Active learning is effective learning

The Internet has opened access to plenty of educational content. However, content itself is not enough to learn and educate ourselves. Ekana upholds active learning as the core of its e-learning experience.


5. Honest insight key to human learning

While the promise of illustrating oneself solely through 10-minute-bite gamification may seem too good to be true, it actually is. In Ekana, we believe in educating people as to how learning occurs so that they can adapt their own strategies and embrace a holistic learning approach.


6. Loving learning means loving deliberate effort

Science-based insight tells us that effort and practice are necessary to achieve mastery of anything. Ekana will be there to make sure you engage in that effort with great motivation and efficacy.


7. Learning is a journey

E-learning anything is a journey across platforms, tools and apps. As any journey, it has ups and downs and features unavoidable contingencies. Ekana will promote a user-centric journey that goes across the web and help you build resilience and strength to overcome any obstacle!


8. Partnerships are our way

We believe that working for education involves getting together, instead of building walls. Ekana will itself collaborate with an ecosystem of partners as part of their ultimate goal: enabling universal human flourishing through education.


9. The limits of IA

Even the smartest IA model will not succeed at replacing the intrinsic fulfillment of knowing we can be of help, and receive help, from a peer human being. Instead, Ekana will put AI in the loop of meaningful connection and cooperation by providing means, tools and support to the experience we seek to have by nature.


10. A user-centered ethics of technology

While the impact of technology depends on the way we use it, technology has an impact on our intentions, beliefs and abilities to choose rightly. Ekana’s ethical design is based on protecting and enhancing the user’s capacities to benefit themselves, their communities and the greater spectrum of human flourishing through collaboration, autonomy and relatedness.
